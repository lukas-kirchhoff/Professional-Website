Enrollment requirements

To be admitted to one of the programmes, you will have to meet certain admission requirements. UBT University of Applied Sciences recognises the following general international qualifications:

Academic requirements

  • International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma or European Baccalaureate Diploma
  • High School diploma from an accredited institution
  • Four GCSE/GCE ‘O’ level passes (final grade A, B or C) and two ‘AS’ level passes. These six passes combined must all be in different subjects.
  • Associate, Bachelor or Master Degree

Language requirements

  • Academic IELTS 6.0 (with a minimum of 5.0 for each skill)
  • Cambridge ESOL score FCE-C (169, or higher)
  • TOEIC 670 (Listening and Reading) and 290 (Speaking and Writing)

Deadline for application

September Start

  • EU/EER Applicants: 15 August
  • Non-EU Applicants: 31 May

How to apply

  1. Go to Studielink.
  2. Log in to your account or create an account if you currently do not have one. You will have access to your own personal Studielink page.
  3. Click on 'Add previous education' (if you have not already done that) and add the previous education that provides admission to the study programme that you wish to follow.
  4. Click on 'Add new enrolment' or 'Enrolment application'.
  5. Select your preferred course and confirm your choice. Select: MA & MSc – dual degree, or the MA – single degree-
  6. Enter the starting month in which you would like to start your programme.
  7. Next, confirm your starting date.
  8. Answer the study programme-specific questions.
  9. Tick the confirmation box and confirm your application.
  10. Enter your payment details for payment of the tuition fees, and confirm again. If you prefer, you can enter this information at a later date, provided you do so before enrolment.
  11. You can track the status of your application on your personal Studielink page.

Once UBT has received your application, you will receive a message from Studielink confirming the following information: The course(s) for which you have applied. Further institutions on proceeding with the enrollment process.
