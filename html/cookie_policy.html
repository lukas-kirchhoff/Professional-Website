Cookie Policy

Cookies
A "cookie" is a piece of information that is stored on your computer's hard drive and which records your navigation of a website so when you revisit that website, it can present tailored options to you based upon the stored information about your last visit. If you don't wish to receive cookies that are not strictly necessary, you may choose to reject such cookies in your browser's privacy settings.

Google Analytics
We measure visitors to our website using Google Analytics. This records what pages you view within our site, how you arrived at our site and some basic information about your computer. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. All of this information is anonymous.

Advertising Programs
We use advertising programs like Google AdWords, Google DoubleClick, Twitter, AdRoll and Facebook. On behalf of these advertising programmes Nhlstenden.com places advertising cookies to give these third parties insights of visitors information on an anonymous basis. These cookies measure if you have seen or clicked on Google AdWords/Doubleclick, Twitter, AdRoll or Facebook adverts.
