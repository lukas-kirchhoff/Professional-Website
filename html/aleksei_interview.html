Why UBT? Aleksei

How is life at the university?

The atmosphere in the university is really friendly and welcoming for international students. As a student from Eastern Europe or as they call it the old world, I was pleasantly surprised by the western Study system where I can learn in practice rather than theory.


What differs from your country and the Netherlands?

I come from Estonia, and I myself am a Ukranian-Estonian. And i can say that people in Netherlands are a lot more open. People even randomly greet you on the streets.


What do you like about the university?

The study system is something that I enjoy the absolute most. The system is friendly to people who have no prior experience in the study.
