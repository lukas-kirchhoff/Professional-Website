Why UBT? Pepe

How is life at the university?

Life in the university here in the Netherlands is nothing as I expected. I come from a small island in the middle of the atlantic ocean. The way of studying is so different but I actually love it! The design based education makes everything easier.


What differs from your country and the Netherlands?

One thing I have noticed as change from my country is the time the shops close which is actually very early compared to Spain.


What do you like about the university?

I like the freedom we have too and also the way we learn. Design based learning is my way of learning as it is a more practical way of studying.


What is interesting about the study you are currently doing?

I love aerospace engeneering just because I love the science between how to fly etc. I find it so interesting and just a beautiful course in general.
