Why UBT? Mihail

How is life at the university?

Life at the university is great, I made a lot of new friends from different countries and cultures, there are fun parties and events all the time and the study process in really interesting.


What differs from your country and the Netherlands?

Most noticeable thing is the language, but luckily a lot of people here speak english. People are more friendlier and its way more peaceful.


What do you like about the university?

I really like that it is one of applied sciences, so the subjects we study are practically oriented ones


What is interesting about the study you are currently doing?

I am studying Banking, because I want to follow in my father footsteps and works in a bank like him. I find everything I learn interesting, there are so many small things and quirks that although complicated sound exciting.
