Omschrijving

Banking is an industry that handles cash, credit, and other financial transactions. Banks provide a safe place to store extra cash and credit. Banking is one of the most important factors in a countries economy, as it provides the liquidity needed for people and businesses to invest for the present and future.

This study will show you the intricacies of the banking world and how you can help shape the future. But why should I pick you?

This is a question that lingers in the mind of many different potential students, we currently have the 3rd best banking course in the Netherlands. We have this rank due to our hard work and learning system, our entire university uses the design based learning (find more about this on Why UBT?) and because we truly care about our students and their future.

We want our students to make a difference in the world no matter the direction they pick.
